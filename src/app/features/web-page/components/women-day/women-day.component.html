Women’s Day Celebration_2023

Cylsys Software Solution Pvt. Ltd. celebrated International Women's Day with great enthusiasm and passion. The theme of the event was “DigitALL: Innovation and Technology for Gender Equality”. “Innovation and technological change, and education in the digital age for achieving gender equality and the empowerment of all women and girls”.



The event was well-attended by employees from different departments. The venue was decorated with colorful balloons, and there was a beautiful flower arrangement that created a cheerful atmosphere..

The perspective of women’s day celebration is women's empowerment which included :

  • Overcoming gender bias and stereotypes in the workplace
  • The importance of mentoring and supporting female colleagues
  • The benefits of work-life balance and flexible work arrangements
  • The need for equal pay and opportunities for women in leadership positions

Women are the Pride of Cylsys



The event also included a fun activity where the participants played games and won prizes. There was also a photo Wall where employees could take pictures with their colleagues and friends.

In conclusion, the event was a huge success, and the female employees of Cylsys Software Solution Pvt. Ltd. appreciated the efforts made by the company to recognize their contributions.
